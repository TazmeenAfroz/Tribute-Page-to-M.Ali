Muhammad Ali

The Champion of Hearts

Muhammad Ali, born Cassius Marcellus Clay Jr., stands as a monumental figure in the world of boxing and beyond. His life, much like a riveting boxing match, unfolded with triumphs, challenges, and an enduring spirit that left an indelible mark on the hearts of millions.

Timeline of Muhammad Ali's Remarkable Journey:

  • 1942: Born in Louisville, Kentucky
  • 1954: Discovers Boxing
  • 1960: Olympic Gold in Rome
  • 1964: Becomes World Heavyweight Champion
  • 1967: Conscientious Objector
  • 1971: Supreme Court Overturns Conviction
  • 1974: "The Rumble in the Jungle"
  • 1975: "The Thrilla in Manila"
  • 1996: Lights the Olympic Flame
  • 2016: Passes Away at 74
